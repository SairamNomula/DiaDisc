About Diabetes


Diabetes mellitus, also referred to as diabetes, is a metabolic condition that raises blood sugar levels. Insulin is a hormone that transports sugar from the blood into your cells where it can be stored or utilised as fuel. When you have diabetes, your body can't use the insulin it does make or doesn't produce enough of it.

Diabetes-related high blood sugar left untreated can harm your kidneys, nerves, eyes, and other organs.

About DiaDisc


DiaDisc makes use of an ML model with a flask backend. To figure out the probability of type-2 diabetes, one uses the DiaDisc, which is essentially a diabetes predictor. The results are pure predictions that were computed using analysis of the data. Our effort is for anyone who want to double-check their reports or who want to know the risk of contracting this ailment before seeking medical attention.
